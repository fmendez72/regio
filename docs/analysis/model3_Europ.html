We will now plot the interactions. There was only 1 signficant interaction between Level and Frames.

Note In the panels of the plot 1= None (no europeanization)

2= Medium europeanization

3= High europeanization

Plotting results

Substantive interpretation

A quick interpreation: The first plot shows that there is probably something going on between levels, but not very clear. The interaction plot shows how the Frame helps to understand whats going on. Key differences:

  1. None category (no Europeanization): Regional level sources much more likely to have no Europeanization in their stories, especially for Economic frame. This makes sense.
  2. Medium Europeanisation: There is also something going on in the middle category; but not worth trying to interpret -although interesting because the lines cross with the interaction when compared to the non-interaction version. Thats why the non-interaction line is rather flat, because it masks the sublte differences related to the Frame.
  3. High Europeanisation: National seems more likely to be Europeanized for Economics, less of change for Politics.
